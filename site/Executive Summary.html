Decisions Needed

Essential elements to scale My Agentic Twin and implement the New Organisation Model.

  • 1
    Budget Alignment

    Confirm interest in investing in My Agentic Twin to unlock the potential value and conduct the roadmap activities, aligning on available budget and collaboration between Business and AI&Data.

  • 2
    Resources Required

    Extending the current core team and increasing development capacities to establish the new organisational model:

    • •Business roles: reinvented processes, value assessment, adoption execution and value realisation.
    • •Cross-functional roles: translating agentic opportunities into detailed business and technical requirements.
    • •AI&Data roles: for agent development and productionisation.
  • 3
    Priorities Alignment

    NPI processes prioritised for My Agentic Twin scope.

  • 4
    Copilot licenses allocated across GSC

    To unlock potential value from My Agentic Twin, Copilot Lite licenses must be allocated to final users in GSC (23,000).
