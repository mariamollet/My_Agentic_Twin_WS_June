01 · Programme Ambition

Turning Time into Value

Scaling agentic AI across GSC to deliver measurable, sustained productivity gains.

Goal
Achieve £18M in hard savings
Equivalent to 1% productivity improvement by scaling agentic AI capabilities across GSK Global Supply Chain. The programme targets 23,000 employees, starting with 3,600 licensed Copilot users.
What it means
2 hours returned per user per week
Out of 2 hours released, 20 minutes need to be turned direct into productivity gains for the £18M target to be fully captured (23K active users in Copilot).
What it takes
Process reinvention and real adoption working in parallel
Reinventing processes or technology alone does not drive change. Real impact comes from the combination of processes, digital tools, and adoption: Processes, People, and Performance.
Agentic Strategy
Enterprise-GSC Agent Accelerator
  • A framework of low-code, modular and customisable agents, reusable across processes and easy to evolve as needs change.
  • Approved technologies in GSK. Until now Copilot was chosen for speed to value, native M365 integration (emails, Teams, SharePoint…), easy engagement, and scalability across GSC.
Democratised agent creation
Self-serving experimentation embedded into everyday work within the Microsoft environment, empowering users to build their own agents rather than depending on a central team.
02 · How We Achieve It

From Strategy to Reality

Programme launched and evolved from idea to real cases, tracking towards a target of 10 use cases by December 2026.

Three priority use cases reinvented end-to-end

Generating high impact, engagement and measurable value. Three areas emerged bottom-up, from mapping where time actually goes and where leaders want it back. The answer was consistent: inefficient meetings and decisions, heavy problem solving, and not enough time developing their people, directly echoing the leadership behaviours GSK already champions: Focus, Motivate, Develop, Care; while simplifying how we work.

Shaped and validated alongside senior leaders across EM and GSC, from site leadership to operational voices closest to the work, the programme has deliberately chosen depth over breadth, concentrating on three use cases that bring Focus to what matters most, Pace to decisions and execution, and Accountability to commitments and outcomes. A strong foundation, built with the right ways of working, before scaling further.

Delivering value by giving time back to people, but also by significantly improving the quality, effectiveness, and consistency of outputs: enabling better meetings and decisions, faster unblocking, deeper and better problem-solving, more DMAICs executed, stronger continuous improvement execution, higher Right First Time ratios, and greater leadership development — embedding a culture of continuous improvement into the way teams work every day.

Behind every agent sits a reinvented process — the way of working is redesigned first, and then powered by 11 agents serving end-to-end multi-agentic flows, together with a network of more than 15 specialised AI-powered workflows handling the underlying work for the reinvented processes around Meetings, Problem Solving and Mentor Twin.

My Agentic Twin - Agent Accelerator Framework
A governed, validated and published ecosystem that enables every GSC employee to build, share, scale and measure the value of agentic solutions.

Opportunity Detection

  • Any GSC employee can identify and propose an idea (not necessarily an agent).
  • Development requires a user with a Copilot licence.

Check the Catalogue & Assess with Twinny

Twinny:

  • Finds similar agents and reuse opportunities
  • Recommends reuse vs build
  • Suggests complexity level (Simple / Medium / Complex)
  • Assesses initial value case: why the agent is needed and what value it will deliver
YES Access existing agent, usage materials, and start working immediately.
NO Development loop is activated.

Assess Complexity & Build

Three parallel paths based on complexity:

SIMPLE

User builds independently in Copilot Light / Agent Builder. Supported by training materials and build guides from the Catalogue.

MEDIUM

AI Ambassador co-builds with the user using Copilot Studio or Copilot Light. AI&Data Team supports industrialisation for scaling.

COMPLEX

AI&Data Team leads full development: pro-code, multi-agent architecture, or integration with cross-functional systems.

AI&Data Clearing House (Gateway)

All agents must pass review and governance before sharing.

  • Quality review and refinement
  • Alignment check against standards and de-prioritised backlog to avoid duplication
  • Governance sign-off including Responsible AI validation

Publish to Agent Catalogue

Only approved agents are published to the Catalogue.

  • Agent factsheet
  • Demo video
  • User guidebook
  • Newsletter mention
  • GSC-wide presentation where relevant

Twinny's catalogue is updated so future users can discover it.

Adoption & Continuous Feedback

  • GSC users adopt the agent and apply new ways of working.
  • Structured feedback (surveys, Ambassador syncs, usage analytics via Copilot Analytics) feeds directly back into new ideas and improvements.

Value Capture & Attribution

Every published agent generates measurable value that feeds back into GSC's performance system.

  • Usage tracked via Copilot Analytics for every published agent
  • Simple value formula translates usage into hours saved and £ value
